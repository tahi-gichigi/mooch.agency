N° 01.1

Janne from Second Home clicked the /soundlikeme card and got a 404. Turns out Notion's custom domain redirect was rewriting the URL to a path that doesn't exist on the Vercel site. Converted the card from a link to a plain div until we build a proper destination. Not ideal, but better than a broken link someone actually tried to use.

1 May 2026
N° 01

Removed per-row case study links from the work list. They were pointing to pages that don't exist yet. Rows are plain divs now. They'll link out again once the case study pages are built.

Also diagnosed why link previews in iMessage show up bare: no OG tags, no raster favicon. The SVG favicon works in browser tabs but messaging apps ignore it. Fix is queued for Phase 4.

29 Apr 2026
N° 00

Soft launch. The whole site is 1 HTML file and about 700 lines of inline CSS. No build step, no framework, no JavaScript. Instrument Serif for the headlines, SF Pro for the body, monospace for the labels. Dark footer, light everything else. Editorial grid layout lifted from the magazines we actually read.

6 case studies in the work list, 6 product cards, a marquee that scrolls client names, and an about/contact section that does the bare minimum. Shipped it before it was ready because that's how you find out what's missing.
