Here's a puzzle. We're made of matter, and matter follows physical laws. Everything in the natural world does. And yet we feel, all the time, that we're free to choose what we do. Both of these seem true. But they look like they can't both be true. If physics decides what your atoms do next, where does the choosing come in? And if you're really choosing, what happened to the physics? People have argued about this for a long time. The question is how to fit the two together.
