There’s an old puzzle about choice. We seem to be made of matter, and matter seems to obey the same laws as everything else in the world. But from the inside, you don’t feel like a rock rolling downhill. You feel as if you can choose what to do.

So which is it? If we’re just matter, how can we be free? And if we’re free, how can we be just matter?

That’s the hard part: to explain how both can be true, or to find out which one has been fooling us.
